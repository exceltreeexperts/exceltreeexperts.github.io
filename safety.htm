Safety

Excel Tree Expert Company has a reputation for being a safe and conscientious company. We have a Certified Tree Care Safety Professional on staff, and all employees are trained in safe operating procedures from their first day on the job.

Training is ongoing, and improving knowledge is an ever present expectation.

We have an excellent safety record with a focus on preventing accidents. Excel follows safety guidelines established by the Treecare Industry Association, OSHA, and ANSI.
