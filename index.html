Excel Tree Experts was founded by Marty Levine, a Certified Arborist, and has grown since the early 1980s. We provide tree care services for residential and commercial clients. As a GSA approved tree company, we have also been trusted with tree management service contracts that have covered all levels of clients from government municipalities.

We provide quality tree care service at a fair price. We believe in Arboriculture as an honorable profession, and encourage the growth and success of our employees, and to educate the public on the merits of Arboriculture. We follow strict safety procedures to protect both our employees and the property of our clients. To ensure we operate within state and federal guidelines, our team holds several tree licenses, safety certifications and belong to numerous arboriculture organizations.

We have long term working relationships wit the National Park Service and the National Arboretum, as well as multitudes of satisfied residential, commercial, and municipal customers.
