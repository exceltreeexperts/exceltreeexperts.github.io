About Excel Tree Experts

Established in 1980 and incorporated in 1985, Excel Tree Experts Co. Inc. is owned and operated by Marty Levine, an arborist certified by the International Society of Arboriculture. Excel Tree Experts Co. Inc. has become one of the most respected full service tree companies in the Baltimore and Washington area.

Excel Tree Experts Co. Inc. is fully licensed to do tree pruning and removal of any type of tree. We have several ISA Certified Arborists and Maryland Licensed Tree Experts on staff. We are very proud of our expert staff, which includes diagnosticians, estimators, tree climbers, crane and bucket truck operators, ground workers, stump grinders, and pesticide and fertilization technicians.

We have worked long term for the National Park Service and the National Arboretum, as well as multitudes of satisfied private, commercial, and municipal customers.

It is never a good idea to have any work done on your property by an unlicensed contractor. We are licensed by the state of Maryland to perform all aspects of tree care. We are fully covered by insurance, and will be happy to provide you with policy information upon request.

A professional will always present you with a written proposal, which you should review carefully before signing and returning to us. We will call you at least one week before your work is to be started.

Depending on the season, you may expect that your work will be done usually four to twelve weeks after you sign and return your proposal.

If you are having pruning done to your trees, we request that you try to be home for part of the day when we do your work. This will ensure that you are happy with the results.

Thank you for giving us the opportunity to be of service to you. We look forward to working for you in the future.
